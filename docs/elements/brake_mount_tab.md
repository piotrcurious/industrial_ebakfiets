# Element: Brake Mount Tab (ISO)

Standardized mounting tabs for hydraulic disc brake calipers.

## Technical Specifications
- **Material**: 6.0mm or 8.0mm Mild Steel.
- **Standards**: ISO 51mm (distance between mounting holes).

## Geometry
- **Mounting Holes**: 2x 6.5mm (Clearance for M6).
- **Hole Spacing**: 51.0mm center-to-center.

## Installation Notes
- Weld to the dropout plate or fork blade.
- **Positioning**: Use a centering jig with the disc rotor installed to ensure the caliper sits exactly over the brake track.
- The tab must be perpendicular to the axle axis.
