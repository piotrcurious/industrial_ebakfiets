# Element: Heavy-Duty Torque Arm

The torque arm prevents the high-torque motor axle (14mm with 10mm flats) from rotating inside the fork dropouts, which would damage the fork and the motor wiring.

## Technical Specifications
- **Material**: 10.0mm Structural Mild Steel (S275 or higher).
- **Length**: 140mm (Center of axle to center of frame anchor).
- **Manufacturing**: Laser-cut.

## Geometry
- **Axle Slot**: 14.2mm circle with 10.2mm parallel flats.
- **Anchor Hole**: 8.5mm (Clearance for M8 bolt).

## Fastener Requirements
- **Anchor**: 1x M8x30mm Grade 10.9 Bolt.
- **Nut**: 1x M8 Nyloc Nut.
- **Washer**: 2x M8 Heavy Duty (Large OD) Washers.

## Installation Notes
- The torque arm must be mounted on the side where the motor cable exits the axle.
- Ensure the anchor bolt is secured to a dedicated 8mm steel tab welded to the fork leg.
- There should be zero play between the motor flats and the torque arm slot.
