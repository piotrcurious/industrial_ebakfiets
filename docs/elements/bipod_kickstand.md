# Element: Bipod Kickstand

A heavy-duty, wide-base kickstand is essential for loading 350kg of cargo without the vehicle tipping.

## Technical Specifications
- **Material**: 25mm x 3.0mm Circular Steel Tubing.
- **Pivot Pin**: 12mm Hardened Steel with integrated grease fitting (Zerk).
- **Base Width**: 400mm (Deployed).
- **Leg Length**: 350mm.

## Manufacturing Notes
- The two legs should be bent at 8 degrees outward to provide a stable footprint.
- Large 60mm diameter "feet" plates should be welded to the bottom of the legs to prevent sinking into soft ground.
- The return mechanism uses dual 2.5mm wire tension springs.

## Installation Notes
- Weld the 10mm steel pivot plate to the center of the cargo bed main spars.
- Ensure the stand clears the chain and the 16-inch rear wheel when retracted.
- Adjust the return spring tension so the stand snaps firmly against the frame during transit.
