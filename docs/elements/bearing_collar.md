# Element: Bearing Locking Collar

The locking collars prevent vertical movement of the 15mm steering shaft within the angular contact bearings.

## Technical Specifications
- **Material**: 4140 Steel or 304 Stainless Steel.
- **Outer Diameter (OD)**: 35.0mm (Matches bearing race OD).
- **Inner Diameter (ID)**: 15.1mm.
- **Thickness**: 15.0mm.

## Geometry
- **Locking Pattern**: 6x M5 threaded radial holes spaced at 60 degrees.

## Fastener Requirements
- **Set Screws**: 6x M5x8mm Grade 12.9 Cup Point Set Screws.

## Installation Notes
- Position the collar directly against the inner race of the 7202 bearing.
- Tighten the 6 set screws in a star pattern to ensure even pressure on the 15mm shaft.
- A small flat ground onto the 15mm shaft at the set-screw locations is recommended for heavy-duty applications.
