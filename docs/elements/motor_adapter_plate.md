# Element: Motor-to-Rim Adapter Plate

The Adapter Plate is the critical interface that bridges the high-torque Golden Motor Magic Pie 5 spoke flange to a standard 13-inch automotive split rim.

## Technical Specifications
- **Material**: 12.0mm Mild Steel (Grade 43) or 10.0mm Stainless Steel (304).
- **Outer Diameter (OD)**: 330mm.
- **Inner Bore (ID)**: 100mm (clearance for motor hub).
- **Manufacturing**: Laser-cut or CNC Waterjet.

## Hole Patterns
1. **Inner Pattern (Motor Interface)**:
   - **PCD**: 320mm (based on Golden Motor spoke flange).
   - **Holes**: 12x 5.5mm (Clearance for M5).
   - **Spacing**: 30 degrees.
2. **Outer Pattern (Rim Interface)**:
   - **PCD**: 260mm.
   - **Holes**: 6x 8.5mm (Clearance for M8).
   - **Spacing**: 60 degrees.

## Fastener Requirements
- **Inner**: 12x M5x20mm Grade 12.9 Socket Head Cap Screws (per plate).
- **Outer**: 6x M8x140mm Grade 10.9 Bolts (shared with rim sandwich).

## Installation Notes
- Apply medium-strength thread locker to all M5 bolts.
- Ensure the motor flange is clean and free of paint where the adapter plate meets to ensure a true mechanical fit.
