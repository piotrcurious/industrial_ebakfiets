# Element: Steering Shaft (Pivot Axis)

The steering shaft is the primary pivot for the front end, transferring torque from the handlebars to the linkage.

## Technical Specifications
- **Material**: 15.0mm Precision Ground Steel Rod (h8 tolerance or better).
- **Length**: 500mm (Standard) - trim to fit.
- **Surface**: Hardened or Chrome-plated to prevent corrosion and wear in the bearing seats.

## Installation Notes
- Ensure the shaft is free of burrs before sliding through the 7202 bearings.
- The shaft is held in place by two Bearing Locking Collars (Top and Bottom).
- Two flats should be machined/ground at the locations of the Bell Crank set-screws to ensure a non-slip mechanical connection.
- Lubricate with heavy-duty lithium grease before final assembly.
