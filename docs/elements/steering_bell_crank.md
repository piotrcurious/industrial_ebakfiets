# Element: Steering Bell Crank

The bell cranks convert the linear motion of the steering tie-rod into rotational motion for the fork and handlebars.

## Technical Specifications
- **Material**: 10.0mm Mild Steel.
- **Effective Length**: 120mm (Pivot center to rod-end center).
- **Manufacturing**: Laser-cut.

## Geometry
- **Main Bore**: 15.1mm (Press-fit or sliding fit on 15mm steering shaft).
- **Rod-End Hole**: 10.2mm (Clearance for M10 rod end).
- **Locking Hole**: 5.0mm radial hole for an M5 set-screw or roll pin to lock the crank to the shaft.

## Fastener Requirements
- **Linkage**: M10x1.5 LH/RH Rod Ends.
- **Lock**: M5x15mm Grade 12.9 Set Screw.

## Installation Notes
- The fork-mounted bell crank and the handlebar-mounted bell crank must be perfectly parallel when the front wheel is pointed straight.
- Use a high-strength thread locker on the M5 locking set-screw.
