# Element: Front Fork Dropout Plate

The front dropouts are the high-stress interface between the fork legs and the M14 motor axle.

## Technical Specifications
- **Material**: 8.0mm Structural Mild Steel.
- **Manufacturing**: Laser-cut.

## Geometry
- **Axle Slot**: 14.2mm (Width) x 25.0mm (Depth). Designed for M14 axle with 10mm flats.
- **Mudguard Mounting**: 1x 6.5mm hole for M6 stay attachment.
- **Brake Mount**: Integrated ISO tabs for 203mm rotor clearance.

## Installation Notes
- Weld to the center of the 30mm wide fork blades.
- Ensure the two dropout slots are perfectly aligned using a 190mm dummy axle during welding.
- Chamfer the entry of the slot for easier wheel installation.
