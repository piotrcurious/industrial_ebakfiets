# Element: Stabilizer Unit (Landing Feet)

The stabilizer units provide additional lateral support during heavy loading/unloading operations, preventing the frame from twisting or tipping when the cargo is off-center.

## Technical Specifications
- **Material**: 35mm x 35mm x 2.5mm Square Steel Tubing (Outer), 25mm x 25mm x 2.5mm (Inner).
- **Drive Mechanism**: M16x250mm Threaded Rod (Grade 8.8).
- **Deployment Range**: 0 - 200mm.

## Manufacturing Notes
- Weld an M16 nut to the top of the inner 25mm tube.
- The outer 35mm tube is welded vertically to the four corners of the cargo bed main spars.
- A 100mm diameter "Elephant Foot" plate should be welded to the bottom of the inner tube to distribute load on soft surfaces.

## Installation Notes
- Ensure the stabilizers are fully retracted before transit to maintain ground clearance.
- Use a standard 24mm wrench or a dedicated handle to deploy the M16 screw.
- Lubricate the threaded rod with heavy marine grease.
