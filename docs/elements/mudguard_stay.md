# Element: Front Mudguard Stay

The stays provide a rigid 3-point mounting system for the oversized 170mm wide front mudguard.

## Technical Specifications
- **Material**: 8mm Round Steel Rod or 10x3mm Flat Bar.
- **Length**: Approx 280mm (Custom fit required based on tire clearance).

## Geometry
- **Eyelet (Dropout side)**: 6.5mm hole for M6 mounting.
- **Eyelet (Mudguard side)**: 5.5mm hole for M5 mounting.

## Fastener Requirements
- **Lower**: 1x M6x16mm Bolt (per stay).
- **Upper**: 1x M5x12mm Bolt (per stay).

## Installation Notes
- Ensure a minimum clearance of 20mm between the tire tread and the mudguard blade.
- The stays should be bent to avoid interference with the 203mm brake rotor and the hub motor housing.
