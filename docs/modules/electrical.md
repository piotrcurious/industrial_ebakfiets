# Electrical Module

The industrial bakfiets uses a decentralized, dual-battery, dual-motor electrical system for maximum efficiency, redundancy, and reduced wiring losses.

## Decentralized Architecture
Instead of a single central battery and controller, the system is split into two localized power units.

### 1. Front Power Unit (Fork Mounted)
- **Location**: Mounted on the custom front fork.
- **Front Battery Retention**: Secured using **2x 20mm rubber-lined stainless steel straps** with M6 T-bolt tensioners.
- **Padding**: 4mm EVA high-density foam at all contact points.

### 2. Rear Power Unit (Frame Mounted)
- **Location**: Mounted on the main frame.
- **Rear Battery Retention**: Secured with identical stainless steel straps and foam padding.

## Dual-Battery Equalizer System
- **Specification**: **8AWG or 10AWG silicone-insulated wire**.

## Cable Management & Routing (Electrical)
To ensure the longest possible life for the electrical system, cables are protected from environmental and mechanical damage.

### 1. Routing Paths
- **High-Current**: Lengths under **500mm**.
- **Impact Protection**: Routed through **welded steel "Cable Bridges"**.

## Tolerances and Safety
- **Retention Force**: Battery mounting straps must withstand **3G vertical acceleration** without shifting.
- **Bend Radius**: Minimum bend radius for power cables is **10x the cable diameter**.
