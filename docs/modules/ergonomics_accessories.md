# Ergonomics and Accessories Module

The operator interface of the industrial bakfiets is designed for safety, adjustability, and comfort during heavy-duty operations.

## Operator Interface
### 1. Seating (Seat and Post)
- **Seat Post**: **31.6mm diameter** aluminum or steel post.
- **Seat Tube Clamp**: **QR (Quick Release) 34.9mm clamp**.
- **Saddle**: Wide, ergonomic industrial saddle with integrated coil spring suspension.

### 2. Steering & Control (Stem and Bars)
- **Handlebars**: 720mm - 780mm width steel riser or downhill (DH) bars for maximum leverage.
- **Stem**: **90mm Adjustable Stem** (0 to 60 degrees) or heavy-duty fixed stem.
- **Grips**: Double-lock-on rubber grips.

## Safety and Accessories
### 1. Lighting System (Lights and Horn)
- **Power Source**: Integrated **48V-to-12V DC-DC Converter**.
- **Headlights**: Dual **10W - 20W LED Floodlights** (IP67/IP69K).
- **Tail-light**: Integrated LED brake and tail-light.

### 2. Audio and Vision (Horn and Mirrors)
- **Horn**: 110dB - 120dB Industrial-grade horn (12V).
- **Mirrors**: Dual **100mm - 120mm Wide-angle Mirrors**.

## Mechanical Integration
### 1. Accessory Mounting
- **Lights**: Mounted using **M6 Grade 8.8 Bolts** to the cargo box steel angle skeleton.
- **DC-DC Converter**: Mounted inside the Rear BLDC Housing Box using **M4 screws**.

## Operation Methods
### Startup Procedure
1. Verify battery levels on the LCD display.
2. Check lighting and horn operation.
3. Ensure stabilizers are fully retracted and secured with hitch pins.
4. Perform a low-speed brake test.

## Tolerances and Fit
- **Seat Post Fit**: **Snug-Fit (0.1mm clearance)** in seat tube.
- **Handlebar Clamp**: Standard **31.8mm** interface.
