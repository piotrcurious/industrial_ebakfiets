# Drivetrain Module

The drivetrain of the industrial bakfiets is designed to provide secondary power and assistance, with a focus on simplicity and extreme durability.

## Pedal Power
### 1. Simple Derailleur System
- **Benefit**: Provides wide gear ratio distribution for different terrains and loads.
- **Why**: Chain tensioning is inherently necessary; a derailleur serves this purpose while also offering gear options.

### 2. Pedal Assist Sensor (PAS)
- **Integration**: Integrated with the electrical system to provide motor support based on pedaling.
- **Why**: Essential for effortless cargo hauling and extending battery range.

## Transmission Components
### 1. Chain and Sprockets
- **Standard**: 1/2" x 1/8" heavy-duty bicycle or industrial-grade chain.
- **Protection**: Full chain guard or industrial-style chain cover to prevent grease on cargo and operator.

### 2. Bottom Bracket and Crankset
- **Standard**: Square taper or BSA-threaded external bearing bottom bracket.
- **Why**: Easily sourced and replaceable with standard bicycle tools.

## Rear Drive Integration
### 1. Rear Hub
- **Integration**: 300W geared hub motor with standard derailleur threads.
- **Tensioning**: Derailleur serves as the chain tensioner for the rear-drive chain.

## Alternative: Belt Drive
- **Benefit**: Lower maintenance, quieter operation, and no grease.
- **Requirement**: A split-frame design or special chainstays to allow the belt to pass through the rear triangle.
