# Drivetrain Module

The drivetrain of the industrial bakfiets is designed for secondary power and assistance, with a focus on simplicity and grease management.

## Pedal Power
### 1. Simple Derailleur System
- **Standard**: Shimano Altus or Deore 7-10 speed.

## Chain Management & Protection
To ensure operator safety and minimize maintenance in industrial environments, the chain is fully managed.

### 1. Chain Tubes
- **Material**: **12mm ID / 16mm OD Heavy-duty HDPE** (High-Density Polyethylene) tubing.
- **Function**: Encases the long return and drive runs of the chain, preventing grease contamination and reducing chain slap against the frame.

### 2. Mounting Clips
- **Design**: 3D-printable or steel-welded clips.
- **Fastening**: Secured to the 40x20mm main frame spars using **M5 Socket Head Screws**.
- **Alignment**: Tubes must be aligned to maintain a maximum 10-degree chain entry/exit angle into the sprockets.

## Transmission Components
### 1. Chain and Sprockets
- **Standard**: 1/2\" x 1/8\" heavy-duty bicycle or industrial-grade chain.

### 2. Bottom Bracket and Crankset
- **Crankset**: 170mm or 175mm length, 42T steel chainring.
