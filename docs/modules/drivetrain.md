# Drivetrain Module

The drivetrain of the industrial bakfiets is designed to provide secondary power and assistance, with a focus on simplicity and extreme durability.

## Pedal Power
### 1. Simple Derailleur System
- **Standard**: Shimano Altus or Deore 7-10 speed.

### 2. Pedal Assist Sensor (PAS)
- **Standard**: 12-magnet sensor.

## Transmission Components
### 1. Chain and Sprockets
- **Standard**: 1/2\" x 1/8\" heavy-duty bicycle or industrial-grade chain.

### 2. Bottom Bracket and Crankset
- **Crankset**: 170mm or 175mm length, 42T steel chainring.

## Cable Management & Routing (Drivetrain)
Efficient gear shifting requires a clean, low-friction Bowden cable run.

### 1. Derailleur Routing
- **Path**: Routed from the handlebar shifter, along the right main frame spar, to the rear derailleur.
- **Guidance**: Use **full-length housing** to prevent dirt ingress, which is common in industrial environments.
- **Entry Angle**: The housing must enter the derailleur with a **large-radius curve (75mm-100mm)** to ensure smooth shifting.

### 2. Protection & Management
- **Conduit**: The shifter housing is bundled with the rear brake line inside a **shared 12mm PA6 corrugated tube** along the frame spar.
- **Vibration Control**: Secured every 150mm with UV-resistant cable ties.
- **Cargo Clearance**: Routed beneath the main frame spars to protect the cable from cargo being loaded or shifted.

### 3. Loop Control
- **Head Tube**: Similar to brake lines, the shifter cable must have a **slack loop** to accommodate steering movement without ghost-shifting or binding.

## Assembly & Disassembly Methods
### Assembly
1. Mount shifter and derailleur.
2. Route full-length housing through the frame bundles.
3. Install the stainless steel inner cable.
4. Adjust indexing for crisp shifting across the range.

## Tolerances and Fit
- **Chain Line Alignment**: Within +/- 3mm.
- **Shifting Clearance**: Cable housing must not touch the chain at any point (Minimum **25mm clearance**).
- **Pedal Clearance**: 100mm ground clearance.
