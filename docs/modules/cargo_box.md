# Cargo Box Module

The cargo box of the industrial bakfiets is designed for maximum utility, standard freight compatibility, and extreme durability in industrial environments.

## Construction Materials
### 1. Main Panels
- **Specification**: **12mm Marine-grade Plywood** (Phenolic resin bonded).
- **Why**: Superior water resistance and structural strength compared to standard plywood.

### 2. Reinforcements
- **Edge Protection**: **25mm x 25mm x 3mm Steel Angle** on all external vertical and bottom horizontal edges.
- **Corner Braces**: Welded steel corner caps (3-way) to prevent panel separation under load.
- **Fasteners**: **M6x40mm Grade 8.8 Carriage Bolts** with large fender washers to distribute stress.

## Dimensions and Compatibility
- **Internal Width**: 810mm.
- **Internal Length**: 1210mm.
- **Internal Height**: 400mm - 600mm (Variable based on user requirement).
- **Standard Load**: Fits one **Standard Euro-pallet (800mm x 1200mm)** with 5mm clearance on all sides.

## Mounting and Security
- **Frame Interface**: The box is secured to the frame spars using **8x M10 Grade 8.8 Bolts** through reinforced mounting points on the frame.
- **Tie-Down Points**: Four integrated **D-ring anchor points** (Zinc-plated steel) rated for 250kg each, bolted through the plywood and steel angle reinforcements.
- **Floor Surface**: Anti-slip rubber matting (3mm) or textured phenolic coating on the plywood.

## Assembly Methods
### Assembly
1. Cut plywood panels to size (Floor: 810x1210mm, Sides/Ends: custom height).
2. Fit steel angle edges to the panels.
3. Drill through angle and plywood; secure with M6 carriage bolts.
4. Weld corner braces to the steel angles for a rigid skeleton.
5. Bolt the entire assembly to the frame using the 8x M10 primary mounts.

### Disassembly
1. Unbolt the 8x M10 frame bolts to remove the entire cargo unit for maintenance or replacement.
