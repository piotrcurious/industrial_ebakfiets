# Weather Protection Module

The industrial bakfiets can be equipped with a modular canopy system to protect cargo and electrical units from environmental conditions.

## Canopy Structure
### 1. Frame
- **Material**: 15mm OD x 1.5mm WT Steel Tubing.
- **Design**: Hooped frame with longitudinal stringers for rigidity.
- **Mounting**: Four corner **M8 Bolt-on Sockets** welded to the exterior of the cargo box skeleton.

### 2. Cover Materials
- **Front Windshield**: **2mm Clear Polycarbonate** sheet, UV-stabilized, bolted to the front hoop.
- **Side/Rear Curtains**: **650gsm Heavy-duty Industrial PVC** (truck tarp quality).
- **Features**: Roll-up side panels with industrial Velcro or heavy-duty snap fasteners.

## Mechanical Integration
- **Socket Interface**: The 15mm frame tubes slide into the M8-locked sockets for easy removal when not required (Modular Design).
- **Drainage**: The canopy is pitched 5 degrees rearward to ensure water runoff away from the operator.

## Assembly Methods
1. Weld the four socket units to the cargo box steel angle edges.
2. Bend 15mm tubing to form hoops.
3. Attach polycarbonate and PVC using industrial adhesives and stainless fasteners.
4. Insert hoops into sockets and lock with M8 bolts.
