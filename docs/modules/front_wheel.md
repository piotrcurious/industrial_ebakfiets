# Front Wheel Module

The front wheel of the industrial bakfiets is designed for heavy-duty cargo transport, utilizing standard automotive parts for durability and ease of maintenance.

## Components
### 1. 13-inch Car Tire (155/70 R13)
- **Standardization**: This is a very common, inexpensive car tire size.
- **Load Capacity**: Rated for approximately 350kg, far exceeding standard bicycle tires.
- **Durability**: High puncture resistance and long service life.

### 2. Two-Part "Split" Rim
- **Design**: Similar to wheelbarrow or Piaggio Ape rims. It consists of two halves bolted together.
- **Maintenance**: Allows for easy tire and tube replacement without the need for specialized tire shop equipment (tire levers, bead breakers).
- **Construction**: Can be fabricated from laser-cut steel plates or modified industrial wheels.

### 3. Front Hub Motor
- **Type**: 250W Direct Drive (Brushless DC).
- **Mounting**: Integrated into the center of the split rim or connected via a reinforced flange.
- **Regenerative Braking**: Direct drive motors allow for electrical braking, reducing wear on mechanical components.

## Technical Considerations
- **Fork Width**: Requires a custom-widened fork to accommodate the 155mm tire width plus clearance.
- **Axle Strength**: Must handle the increased weight and torque; 14mm or 20mm hardened steel axle is recommended.
- **Mudguard**: Industrial-style wide steel or plastic fender.
