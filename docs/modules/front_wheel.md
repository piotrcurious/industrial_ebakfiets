# Front Wheel Module

The front wheel of the industrial bakfiets is designed for heavy-duty cargo transport, utilizing standard automotive parts for durability and ease of maintenance.

## Components
### 1. 13-inch Car Tire (155/70 R13)
- **Dimensions**: 155mm Width, 548mm Diameter.
- **Load Rating**: 350kg.

### 2. Two-Part "Split" Rim
- **Design**: Two halves bolted together (industrial type).
- **Bolt Pattern**: 6x270mm PCD.
- **Fasteners**: **6x M8x140mm Grade 10.9 Bolts** with heavy-duty washers and **M8 Nyloc nuts**. (Tighten to 25-30Nm). These bolts sandwich both rim halves and both motor flanges for maximum rigidity.

### 5. Front Mudguard Assembly
- **Main Blade**: High-impact ABS or Aluminum (170mm width).
- **Crown Mounting**: Heavy-duty offset bracket secured to the fork crown with **2x M6x20mm Grade 10.9 Bolts**.
- **Stay Mounting**: Dual 8mm steel stays connecting the mudguard sides to the fork dropouts via **2x M5x12mm Bolts**.

### 3. Inner Tube
- **Specification**: 13-inch automotive tube with Schrader valve.

### 4. Front Hub Motor: Golden Motor Magic Pie 5 (Fatbike Edition)
- **Type**: Direct Drive BLDC with internal Sine-wave controller.
- **Diameter**: 320mm (Spoke flange), 310mm (Housing).
- **Axle**: 14mm with 10mm flats, **190mm Dropout Spacing** (Fatbike standard).
- **Axle Fasteners**: **2x M14x1.5 Flange Nuts** with **2x M14 High-Torque Washers** (tabbed).
- **Braking**: 203mm Disc Rotor with 6-bolt ISO mount (44mm PCD).
- **Torque Arm**: 10mm laser-cut steel plate (10.2mm cutout) secured to the fork leg using **1x M8x30mm Grade 10.9 Bolt and Nut**.

### 5. Motor-to-Rim Adapters
- **Design**: 2x 12mm Brass or Steel adapter plates.
- **Interface**: Bridges the 56-hole motor spoke flange to the 6-bolt rim pattern.
- **Fasteners (Inner)**: 12x M5x20mm Grade 12.9 bolts per side.
- **Fasteners (Outer)**: 6x M8x140mm bolts (shared with rim assembly).

## Mechanical Integration & Assembly
### 1. Front Power Housing (Fork Mounted)
- **Housing Mounting**: Oversized BLDC box secured to fork tabs using **4x M6x16mm Stainless Steel Hex Bolts** and rubber vibration dampers.

## Assembly & Disassembly Methods
### Assembly
1. Deflate tube and sandwich tire/tube between rim halves.
2. Align the assembly with the Hub Motor flanges (Sandwich: Flange-Rim-Rim-Flange).
3. Pass 6x M8x140mm bolts through the pattern. Tighten to **30Nm**.
3. Slide axle into dropouts.
4. Install torque arm on the axle and bolt to fork leg.
5. Tighten M14 axle nuts to **50Nm**.

### Disassembly
1. Fully deflate tire.
2. Unbolt M14 axle nuts and M6 torque arm bolt.
3. Remove wheel and unbolt M8 rim bolts.

## Tolerances
- **Axle-to-Dropout**: 0.2mm clearance (10.2mm slot for 10.0mm flats).
- **Fastener Grade**: Grade 10.9 required for rim and axle-related hardware.
