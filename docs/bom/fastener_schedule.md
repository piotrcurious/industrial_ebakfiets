This document provides a consolidated list of all fasteners required to assemble the Industrial E-Bakfiets. Using the specified grade is critical for structural safety.

## 3. General Requirements
- **Nyloc Nuts**: All through-bolts must be secured with Nyloc (nylon-insert) locking nuts.
- **Washers**: Use heavy-duty, thick washers on all structural joints to prevent the bolt head from crushing the steel tubing.
- **Loctite**: Apply Medium Strength (Blue) thread locker to any fastener subject to motor or road vibration that does not use a Nyloc nut.
